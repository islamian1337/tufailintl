About Us

TUFAIL INTERNATIONAL is a Pakistan based Recruiting agency specialized in Overseas Employement Promotion. The company has the most experienced individuals in its team who are veteran in Overseas Employement, Human Resource Management, Processing VISAs and other relevant affairs. Working under License No: MPD/2140, issued by Federal Ministry of Manpower and Overseas Pakistanis,Tufail INTERNATIONAL is moving fast towards the status of most vibrant and reliable Overseas Employement promoters of Pakistan.
